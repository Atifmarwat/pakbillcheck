MEPCO Online Bill Check - Quick Guide

MEPCO Online Bill Check - Enter your 14-digit reference number from your MEPCO electricity bill to instantly view, download and print your duplicate bill. This free tool helps you access your MEPCO bill online anytime, anywhere.

Simply enter your 14-digit reference number in the tool above, select your area type (Urban/Rural), and click "Check MEPCO Bill Online". Your bill will be ready in seconds.

Quick Tip: Your 14-digit reference number is printed on every MEPCO bill. Keep it handy for instant duplicate bill download.
